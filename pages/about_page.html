About Us

Our team is always getting bigger, but we all work toward
the same goal tomake sales success not only possible, but also
inevitable for all terms.
AGRIसखा is a private limited company that was established
on September 3, 2021. A is an AGRIसखा enterprise providing
an online marketplace to connect farmers and Agri input sellers,
where farmers can buy branded, high-quality, and original products
through AGRIसखा. We are a company that delivers the highest quality
input and guidance to farmers for their growth. Our services are
available in over 26,000 locations. Through our services, we are
helping farmers to have better profitability.



Our Mission

Our objective is to enable the best farming methods
to be implemented in order to promote healthier
communities through the use of novel food cultivation
practices while remaining true to our heritage. Through
the use of outreach models, we hope to become an end-to-end,
sustainable integrator, bringing together cutting-edge
technology and worldwide best practices at a reasonable price.



Our Vision

Helping Farmers. Fighting climate change.
Sustainability is at the heart of our company and
our many partnerships and collaborations. Our Good
Growth Plan drives the work we do and measures the
difference we make, demonstrating how we contribute to
the Sustainable Development Goals.
